Challenge Pit

The structure of a clan hiearchy is always in question. The challenge pit provides the perfect place to usurp your least favorite clan leader. The inspirational presence of the challenge pit increases the strength of all clan members on the same level. The upkeep cost of all clan members who do not have leader roles is also decreased by the presence of a challenge pit.

Effects that can be gained from a Challenge Pit:

  • The strength of all clan members on the same level is increased.
  • The upkeep cost of all clan members without leader roles is decreased.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)
