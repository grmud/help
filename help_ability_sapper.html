Summary: Abilities: Builder Special Abilities: Demolitions


 Demolitions

Sappers deal with explosives, quick tunnelling, and the destruction of structures and items. The sapper ability will give the builder access to the use of explosives and other tools which can be used for quick, dirty work.

Command: destroy <here|direction>

This ability is based on:

  • Agility
  • Energy

    • Effects of the demolitions ability:

    • Demolitions allows the monster to do direct damage to a lair structure or den.
    • The grunts of a monster with this ability will destroy structures at a faster rate.
