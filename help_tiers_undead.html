Undead Tier

Wandering undead monsters begin their lives as spirits. As they acquire karma and reincarnate, they travel down a long path, becoming larger and stronger with each step. They will soon outstrip their social brethren in power and skill. Each new monster type down the tier will have special features not available to other monsters.

  1. Spirit
  2. Ghost or Zombie
  3. Specter
  4. Wight or Haunt
  5. Revenant
  6. Banshee or Afreet
  7. Nightmare or Dracolich*

*Note: At tier level seven, an afreet may only reincarnate as a dracolich, and a banshee may only reincarnate as a nightmare. Such is the power of these monsters that only one player may be a dracolich, and only one player may be a nightmare.
