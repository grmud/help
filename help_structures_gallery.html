Gallery

A gallery is a place where monsters gather to share their knowledge of the world and to tell stories. This general increase in emphasis on knowledge and mythology increases the favor point gain of shamans and builders. Clan grunts are put in a good mood by the presence of the gallery and build at a faster rate while on the same level as it. When enough favors are invested in the gallery, items may be brought here to be identified, giving the clan detailed information about them.

Effects that can be gained from a Gallery:

  • The favor gain of all clan shaman and builders is increased.
  • Grunts on the same level as the gallery build faster.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)

Commands usable at a Gallery:

  • identify -- Identifying an item will give you information about it.

Caste-specific Names:

Humanoid: Cave Painting Gallery
Chimera: Storyteller's Cave
