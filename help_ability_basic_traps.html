Summary: Abilities: Builder Special Abilities: Basic Traps


 Basic Traps

A large part of lair defense is the building and maintaining of traps. Builders with this ability get access to the twelve basic trap types. As they advance in this skill, their traps will be more effective and difficult to disarm.

Command: set <item|portal|exit|here> [with] <trap name>

This ability is based on:

  • Agility
  • Energy
