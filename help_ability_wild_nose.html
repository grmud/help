Summary: Abilities: General Special Abilities: Wild Nose


 Wild Nose

Monsters live in a world of smells. Monsters with this ability can track other creatures by following their scent trails. They can also identify the world around them by detecting the individual odors of creatures and items.

Command: N/A

This ability is based on:

  • Authority
  • Energy

    • Blood Oath | Creeping Crud | Fortitude | Venom
