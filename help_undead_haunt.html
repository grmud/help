Summary: General Mechanics: Gameplay Mechanics: Castes and Races: Undead: Haunt


Haunts are the embodiment of fear. They appear as dark figures, with a cloak of shadows wrapped around a skeletal body. Haunts are bathed in an aura of horror, which causes all those around them to be overwhelmed with fear. Haunts have the ability to inhabit the body of another, taking control of their actions while the helpless victim watches in bound paralysis.
