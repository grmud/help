Summary: Abilities: Humanoid Special Abilities: Magic Resistance


 Magic Resistance

Humanoid brains are just not suited well to the arcane arts. This ability allows humanoids to resist magic simply by force of will. All the monster with this ability has to do is to not believe that magic exists, and they will escape the affects of any magic cast on them.

Command: N/A

This ability is based on:

  • Strength
  • Authority
