Grunts

Each caste has a type of NPC grunt that will do menial tasks for you, if you are a social monster, that you and most other monsters would never consider doing. However, that's why you can command your grunt cousins. Grunts follow your commands, obey you and so forth and do other tasks depending on your role. However, to acquire a grunt, you will have to create it or summon it.

  • Undead clans produce Skeleton grunts
  • Humanoid clans produce Goblin grunts
  • Chimera clans produce Harpy
