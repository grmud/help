Tyrant

The tyrant is a harsh leader, ruling with an iron fist. He demands all from his Minions, and few dare speak against his will. While this leads to increased the offensive capabilities of his minions -- as afraid of their Leader's wrath as the enemies talons -- it also means that they are more difficult to keep happy in such a stressful environment.

  • Tyrants gain an increase in size.
  • The offensive capabilities of a Tyrant's minions are increased.
  • Tyrants gain an increase to their authority.
  • Tyrants must pay a higher upkeep cost for their minions.
