Sleeping Chamber

A sleeping chamber is a monster's own personal room within a lair. Any resources in a sleeping chamber will be counted towards the owning monster's personal hoard.

Effects that can be gained from a sleeping chamber:

  • Owner may have a personal hoard.

Caste-specific Names:
Undead: Personal Tomb
Humanoid: Sleeping Chamber
Chimera: Covert

Building Costs:
Gold: 300
Stone: 600
Caste: 850

Maintenance Costs:
