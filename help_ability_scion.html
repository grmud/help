Summary: Abilities: Leader Special Abilities: Scion


 Scion

The scion ability grants a monster an intimate connection with Grendel, the master of all monsters. Through this connection, the scion is better able to perform acts of heroic leadership. The monster with this ability will gain more of Grendel's favor for using leadership abilities. This favor bonus will also trickle down to any minions underneath the monster.

Command: N/A
