Summary: Abilities: Shaman Special Abilities: Ritual


 Ritual

A ritual is a clan-wide event, involving as many members as possible in directing the clan's activities. A shaman casting a ritual will decide on what type of ritual will be performed. Different types of rituals will provide different types of bonuses to all members of the clan. The strength of the ritual is determined by the number of participants and the power of the shaman.
