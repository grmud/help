Training Room

The training room provides a place for warriors and warrior grunts to train their combat skills and practice combat. A training room increases the strength and agility of all single role warriors in the clan. It also increases the morale of all grunts whose masters are warriors or magical monsters. Training rooms also increase the damage done by all clan warriors using tooth and nail.

Effects that can be gained from a Training Room:

  • The strength and agility of all clan single role warriors is increased.
  • The morale of all grunts underneath magical or warrior clan members is increased.
  • The damage of warrior tooth and nail attacks is increased.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)
