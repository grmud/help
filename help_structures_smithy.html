Smithy

A smithy is a humanoid only structure that improves the effectiveness of weapons and armor. Smithies provide increases in effectiveness for the weapon skills of all clan members. They also improve the damage of all clan attacks that use weapons, and increase the effectiveness of all clan members' armor while they are inside of the lair. Smithies also increase the iron storage capacity of all clan storage rooms.

Effects that can be gained from a Smithy:

  • The effectiveness of all clan members' weapon skills is increased.
  • The damage of all clan attacks using weapons is increased.
  • The effectiveness of all clan members' armor is increased while in the lair.
  • The iron storage capacity of all storage rooms in the lair is increased.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)
