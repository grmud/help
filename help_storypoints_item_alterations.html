Summary: StoryPoints: Regular Subscribers: Item Alterations


Custom Item or Item Alteration

  • Available to: Premium & Regular Subscribers
  • Requires GM Support
  • Cost: 250 SPs
For those special items that are rarely sold, if ever, this option gives you the opportunity to customize the description of an item designed to your specifications. However, any such request must be consistent with the gameworld. For example, a request for a Laser Crossbow would be rejected. Examples of a customized item description could be: a dragonbone necklace, a bow strung with Grimalkin gut, or a jeweled mace. All items subject to the discretion of the GM.
