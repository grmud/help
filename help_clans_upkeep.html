Upkeep Costs

Every social monster has an influence. Influence is determined by a number of factors. The biggest factors that increase influence are the level of a monster's minions, and the size of both the master and the minion's hoard sizes. Clan leaders also gain influence based on the size of their lair. Each monster also has an upkeep cost, which is based on that monster's influence. Influence cost will be in gold and the caste-specific resource of the monster's caste (Iron for humanoids, Mana for chimera, and Shadow for undead). This influence cost must be paid to a monster's minions on a period basis. The amount paid to minions may be modified by setting a generosity level. Monsters are only responsible for paying the upkeep cost of minions directly underneath them in the clan hierarchy. Upkeep cost will be paid directly from the clan's hoard.
