Slime Pit

The slime pit is the Humanoids' play room. The pit gives them the ability to cover themselves in muck, and gives off a putrid smell. However, the slime that covers your body, provides you with an extra coat of protective defense, while the pungent smell gives you that extra boost of strength needed to take on your foes. You are prevented from bathing too often in the slime pit as its effects can be toxic.

Effects that can be gained from a slime pit.

  • When a humanoid bathes at its clan's slime pit, it gains an increase to its strength for a short period of time.
  • By investing favor into this structure, it increases the speed at which grunts will mine iron.
  • By investing more favor into this structure, all humanoids bathing at the pit will be granted a protective coating that helps greatly when they are engaged in combat.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)

Commands:

  • bathe -- this will instill the effects mentioned above on you for a short period of time.
