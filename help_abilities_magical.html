Summary: Abilities: Magical Special Abilities


Magical Special abilities

Magical monsters are unique creatures that can tap into the elemental powers of the world and their special abilities reflect this. When Uglies think of monsters, monsters with magical abilities are often the first that come to their minds because of their awesome display of supernatural power.

Magical monsters' powers are generally more destructive than a Shaman and only available to the Undead and Chimera, if they have taken a magical role.

Chain Lightning Cold Touch
Cold-breathing Disease Breath
Fire-breathing Fireball
Fly Focus
Haste Poison Breath
Raise Regenerate
Resonance Resurrection
Shroud of Mist Spit Acid
Stun Teleport
True Sight
