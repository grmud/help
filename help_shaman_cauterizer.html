Cauterizer

Cauterizers are great practicers of triage, renowned for instinctively knowing what can best be done for their patients at first glance. They gain bonuses to their healing abilities, and their Minions gain an increase in their Energy, though at the expense of their natural armor.

  • Cauterizers get a bonus to the use of the heal. ability
  • Cauterizers gain a bonus to their energy.
  • Cauterizers and their minions have a penalty applied to their defense.
