Kobold

Kobolds are small, sneaky creatures. They are short, thin humanoids, completely bald except for the tufts of hair that grow out their ears and nose. They have long feet, which end in sharp claws. They are natural archers, preferring to hide in the shadows and attack their victims from a distance. Kobold are mean spirited and delight in tormenting larger creatures, dancing just out of their reach and peppering them with a hail of missiles.

  • Kobolds are of small size.
  • Kobolds are resistant to slashing, crushing, and piercing damage.
  • Kobolds are vulnerable to poison, acid, and disease.
  • Kobolds have a general penalty applied to their offense and defense.
  • Kobolds have a penalty applied to their authority and agility.
