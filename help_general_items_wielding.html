Summary: General Mechanics: World Interaction: Item Manipulation: Wielding


Weapons can be grasped in one or two hands. If one-handed, you may then use a shield or a second weapon (if you have the special ability) in the other hand. However, if you wield a two-handed weapon, you cannot use a second weapon or a shield.

Some common commands that pertain to wielding weapons:

  • unwield <weapon|shield>
  • wield <weapon|shield>
