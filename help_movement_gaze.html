Gazing and landmarks.

You may use the gaze command to look around your general area, and find any nearby landmarks. Some landmarks, in particular Grendel's Shrine, can be seen from a very great distance. Other landmarks can be seen from varying distances. Landmarks are a very useful way to find your bearing while travelling overland.

Syntax: 'gaze'

Example of gazing: You gaze about. Sharp talons reach out of a grove of trees off to the east, marking the presence of Grendel's Shrine.
