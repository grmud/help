Summary: Backstory: Places: Big Finger


The Ogre King Ugonnadie, the longest reigning ogre king in all history, had a monument erected in the second and final week of his reign. Perched on the edge of the continent and facing the homeland of the humans, it was originally a giant, open ogre palm. It was designed to appear to be waving, showing the delicious pink skins that they were very much welcome here. In the years since it was first built, most of its structure has eroded, leaving only a hand with a single, solitary finger pointing upwards and towards the pink skin homelands.
