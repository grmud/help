Grave Robber

Only undead builders can be grave robbers. Grave robbers are able to build a unique building, an Immortality Tomb, which allows a monster to sleep briefly within it and gain short-term bonuses to all their abilities. Corpses may also be desecrated at an immortality tomb to gain karma. They are particular good at gathering Shadow, but slept through most of their healing and assembly classes, and so find these buildings particularly expensive to construct.

  • Grave Robbers with the basic structures ability may build Immortality Tombs.
  • The grunts of Grave Robbers mine shadow at an enhanced rate.
  • Grave Robbers pay an increased cost to build certain structures.
