Saw-bones

While it is sometimes said that if you are left to the ministrations of a Saw-bones you might as well be dead already, this is quite unfair. The fear of the medical attention they might receive does inspire the clan to be more defensive than they otherwise might though, giving them a bonus to their natural armor, as well as giving the Saw-bones bonus resistance to all types of damage. There is some truth to the idea that a Saw-bones just isn't as good a healer as they might be though, with they and all their Minions Healing Ability somewhat decreased.

  • Saw-bones take reduced damage from slashing, piercing, and crushing damage.
  • Saw-bones have a penalty applied to the use of the heal ability
