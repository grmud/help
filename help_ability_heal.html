Summary: Abilities: Shaman Special Abilities: Heal


 Heal

Monsters with the heal ability can close the wounds of other creatures, allowing them to continue on to fight and rampage another day. Through the hand of Grendel the shamen is granted the ability to heal the physical wounds of others.

Command: heal <character>

This ability is based on:

  • Energy
  • Authority

    • Effects of the heal ability:

    • Heal adds hit points to the target.
    • Adding ranks to heal will increase the amount of hit points recovered.
