Summary: Abilities: Magical Special Abilities: Raise


 Raise

The raise dead ability can be used on a nearby corpse to animate its bones and create a skeleton grunt. This grunt can then be incorporated into the clan work structure.

Command: animate <corpse> <grunt|warrior>

This ability is based on:

  • Energy
    • Raise may only be purchased by members of the undead caste.

    Despots may purchase the raise ability for half of the normal karma cost.

    Effects of the raise ability:

    • Raise will create a skeleton grunt from a corpse. You must still pay the normal shadow cost for summoning a skeleton grunt, but do not need to pay the gold cost.
    • Grunts animated with raise do not have a master. You must invite the grunt as usual.
