Teleporter

By harnessing powerful magic, teleporters allow clan members a convenient way to get from one place to another quickly. Newly built teleporters can hold one destination, but older, sturdier ones have been known to hold several.

Commands Usable at a Teleporter:

  • transcribe <marked location> - This will add a destination to the teleporter.
  • teleport <teleporter marked location> me - Teleports the monster to the destination mark.
