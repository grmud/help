Spirit

Spirits are cold souls, fields of negative energy which infest the physical plane. They carry with them part of another, slower world where time has less meaning. They are capable of creating ties to this world, transforming portions of it into shadow, infecting their victims with a slow touch. Spirits wander this world, hunting out beings whose souls they can drag back to their own.

  • Spirits are of small size.
  • Spirits may purchase the cold touch ability at half of the normal karma cost.
  • Spirits are extremely resistant to cold damage.
  • Spirits are vulnerable to electric and fire damage.
  • Spirits have a general penalty applied to their offense and defense.
  • Spirits have a penalty applied to their strength and agility.

Spirits may reincarnate to the following monster types:
