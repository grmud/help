Summary: Commands: Assist


Usage:  assist <message>

Sends a request for assistance. After you have sent an assist, a GameMaster or StoryGuide will contact you.

Note that the Worlds Apart GameMasters are only available during daytime hours. Also note that GameMasters or StoryGuides may be available, but not listed on 'who'.

Please also keep in mind that assists should be used for Out Of Character assistance.
