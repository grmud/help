Summary: Abilities: Shaman Special Abilities: Fortify


 Fortify

The fortify ability allows a shaman to strengthen the natural healing of any creature. Creatures who have been fortified will recover from damage at a much greater rate than normal.

Command: fort?ify <character>

This ability is based on:

  • Energy
  • Authority

    • Effects of the fortify ability:

    • Fortify increases the healing rate of the target.
    • Adding ranks to fortify increases the healing bonus and the duration of the effect.
