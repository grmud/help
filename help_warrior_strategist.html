Strategist

The Strategist specialises in co-ordinating the attacks of grunts and other combatants. Their superior tactical skills give all combatants, grunt and Minion alike, bonuses to their attacks when fighting with the Strategist, but reduce the agility of the strategist as it concentrates on coordination.

  • Strategists receive a penalty to their agility.
  • Strategists, their grunts, and their minions gain a bonus to their to-hit chances.
