Summary: Abilities: Warrior Special Abilities: Boulder Throw


 Boulder Throw

Humanoid monsters that are size Huge or larger, may acquire this ability. A boulder-throwing monster can hurl boulders from a distance at other creatures smashing them into the ground.

Command: hurl boulder [at] <target>

This ability is based on:

  • Strength
  • Agility
    • Command Summary
    • hurl boulder at <target> -- Allows you to hurl a boulder at the specified target.
    • search -- Allows you to search the current area for a boulder (some areas will be more likely to have boulders than others).
    • scan <direction> -- Allows you to scan around you for targets at which to hurl boulders, <direction> is one of the cardinal directions (n,nw,ne,s,se,sw,e,w).
