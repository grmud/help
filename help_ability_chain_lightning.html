Summary: Abilities: Magical Special Abilities: Chain Lightning


 Chain Lightning

The use of the chain lightning ability creates a lightning bolt which bounces from target to target, inflicting shock damage to all it touches.

Command: lightning <target>

This ability is based on:

  • Energy
    • Chain Lightning may only be purchased by monsters that have magical as their only role.

    Effects of the chain lightning ability:

    • Chain lightning is a long ranged attack that does electric damage. At high levels, it can damage more than one target.
    • Investing favors in chain lightning will increase its damage and add the ability to splash to additional targets.
