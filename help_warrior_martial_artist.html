Martial Artist

The Martial Artist is a terror in combat, eschewing weapons and armor for the freedom that their natural weapons give. Their training gives them and their immediate Minions bonuses to both offense and defense in combat, but they cannot use any weapons or armor. Chimera cannot be Martial Artists.

  • Martial Artists gain bonuses when using the tooth and nail ability.
  • Martial Artists gain a natural defensive bonus.
  • Martial Artists cost more in upkeep.
  • Martial Artists cannot wear armor or use weapons.
