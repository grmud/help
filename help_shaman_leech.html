Leech

Leeches deal in transfer of life-force, draining it from one source and redirecting it to another. Leeches are able to use their art to increase the effectiveness of their healing when they heal other Undead. They are particularly ineffective at healing Humanoids though, sometimes being accused of sucking the life out of them while they ostensibly heal their patients. Leeches and their Minions are particularly resistant to degenerative damage.

  • Leeches are very good at using the heal ability on undead.
  • Leeches are not very good at using the heal ability on humanoids.
  • Leeches and their minions are resistant to disease, poison, and acid damage.
