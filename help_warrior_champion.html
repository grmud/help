Champion

Champions specialize in one-on-one and ritual combat. They are loners, living for the glory of single combat, and cannot have Minions. Their hatred for particular types of enemy gives them bonuses when fighting those races, and they are superior in individual combat.

  • Champions may purchase the blood oath ability for half of the normal cost in karma.
  • Champions receive a bonus to the damage they do.
  • Champions may not have minions.
