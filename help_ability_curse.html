Summary: Abilities: Shaman Special Abilities: Curse


 Curse

Using this ability, the shaman is able to curse an object or creature. Cursed objects will have highly negative affects on their users, yet these same users will not be able to get rid of the accursed object, due its strong pull over their will. Cursed creatres will slowly deteriorate in all areas over time.

Command: N/A
