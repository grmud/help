Summary: Abilities: Undead Special Abilities: Corpse Feast


 Corpse Feast

Undead monsters with the corpse feast can consume the lifeless bodies of creatures. Eating a corpse with this ability allows the monster to regain lost health, and also to cure some disease and poison.

Command: feast [on] <corpse>

This ability is based on:

  • Energy
  • Authority

    • Corpse feast may only be purchased by members of the undead caste.

    Ghouls may purchase the corpse feast ability for half of the normal karma cost.

    Effects of the corpse feast ability:

    • Feasting on a corpse will heal a monster and cure some poison or disease if the monster is poisoned or diseased.
    • Adding ranks to corpse feast will increase the amount healed and the amount cured.

    Corpse Feast | Noncorporeal | Drain Life | Raise
