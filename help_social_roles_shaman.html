Summary: General Mechanics: Gameplay Mechanics: Social Monsters: Roles: Shaman


Shaman are the healers and diviners of the monster world, vitally assisting in patching up those wounded in combat with their enemies, as well as those injured in the occasional back tunnel brawl. They do more than heal however, assisting in numerous other ways to make the life of a monster more pleasant than it might be otherwise often through the use of protection and damage reduction. A shaman's primary attributes are energy and authority.

There are a number of subtypes to the Shaman role available to all Shaman regardless of race:

  • Empath
  • Cauterizer
  • Hierophant
  • Saw-bones

In addition, each race has a Shaman subtype available only to them:

  • Humanoid Witch Doctor
  • Chimera Wound Licker
  • Undead Leech
