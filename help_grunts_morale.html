Grunt Morale

Even though grunts are not subject to boredom and follow you unquestionly, they still have morale. A grunt's morale mostly affects how comfortable it is in a given situation or whether or not it is willing to fight. If a grunt's morale drops low enough it will run away to the entrance of your clan's lair, or to Grendel's Shrine, where it will find peace and solace. So be careful and make sure you protect your grunt from the attacks of adventurers and minions. Grunts also prefer the comfort of their lair, and they are encouraged by the presence of leaders.
