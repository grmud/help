Tiers

As a wandering monster, you have the ability to reincarnate into more powerful creatures with greater and more abilities. The level of ability and power your wandering monster is represented by a tier. There are seven, more progressively powerful, tiers to which you can reincarnate. Your caste limits the race of monster available to you at each tier, but there can be more than one monster race per a tier for each caste.

Reincarnating into a higher tier has many advantages. As you traverse on the wandering monster tiers upwards, you will find that your monster will generally get larger. Larger monsters can hold more and have access to abilities that other smaller monsters do not. Certain special abilities are also only available to larger monsters, such as the devastating Stomp Em ability, and the ability to throw boulders. Your tier also determines how high you can raise your attributes, the number of special abilities you can have, and maximum rank for those special abilities.

At the end of each wandering monster tier is the ultimate monster of that type. These monsters are so powerful that there can only be one of them in existence at one time.
