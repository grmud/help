Diplomat

The diplomat is the master negotiator, salesman estrordinaire, able to sell fire to efreeti and persuade dragons to lie down with uglies. Soothing their natural antipathies, Diplomats are able to have more than one caste in their clan.

  • Diplomats may have more than one caste of monsters in their clan.
