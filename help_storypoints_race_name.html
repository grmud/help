Summary: StoryPoints: Premium Subscribers: Custom Race Name


Custom Character Race Name

  • Available to: Premium Subscribers
  • Command Usage: name me <adjective>
  • Cost: 250 Story Points
You can spend Story Points to choose an adjective that will be put in front of your monster race as part of your name. For example: Tripe the Swamp Troll, or Seta the Fire Salamander. However, the adjective you choose must be less than fifteen characters, may only be one word. Use the command mentioned above to make the change.

Worlds Apart Productions retains the right to edit or remove the name adjective if it is abusive, racial, prejudice, vulgar, or offensive. If you do use an adjective that Worlds Apart does deem as inappropriate, we will remove the adjective, and you will not receive a refund for the spent Story Points.
