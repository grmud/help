Summary: General Mechanics: Gameplay Mechanics: Castes and Races: Humanoid: Dwerger


The dwerger is a foul smelling dwarf who comes from the earth, a relative of the troll. Their skin is the color of earth, their bodies are always dirty, their hair stringy and mudded. Dwergers come from a world of darkness and pressure. They are masters of crushing and smashing, and love to break things, bringing their parts home to mother earth.
