Summary: General Mechanics: Gameplay Mechanics: Castes and Races: Humanoid: Troglodyte


Troglodytes are pale skinned reptilian creatures who dwell deep within the earth. They have large eyes adapted to the darkness, and sharp teeth which poke out from their mouths. They have small nostril slits and are completely devoid of hair. Troglodytes have grown strong from constant competition for underground resources, but their lack of contact with outsiders has made them vulnerable to the diseases of the outer world.
