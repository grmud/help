Leader Role

Leaders are the generals and managers of the monster world. While anyone can have Minions, they are the only role that can form or lead a clan. They are also able to command grunts to undertake guarding and patrolling activities. They primarily require authority and strength. Leaders have a beneficial effect upon Minions and grunts fighting in their immediate area, though how great an effect depends on the leader in question.

There are a number of subtypes to the leader role, available to all Leaders regardless of race:

  • Tyrant
  • Alpha
  • Chieftain
  • Matriarch
  • Diplomat

In addition, each race has a leader subtype available only to them:

  • Undead Despot
  • Chimera Sovereign
