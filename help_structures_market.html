Market

A market is a gathering place where monsters can get together, make trades, buy and sell, and improve their economic skills. The presence of a market in a lair increases the resource carrying capacity of all clan members. Markets also increase the speed at which grrunts mine stone, and increase the maximum stone storage capacity of all storage rooms.

Effects that can be gained from a Market:

  • The resource carrying capacity of all clan members will be increased.
  • Clan grunts will mine stone at a faster rate.
  • All storage chamber's stone storage capacity will be increased.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)

Caste-specific Names:
Undead: Bone Bazaar
Humanoid: Orc Emporium
Chimera: Greenmarket
