Summary: General Mechanics: Gameplay Mechanics: Social Monsters: Roles: Builders


Builders are responsible for the architecture and construction of a clan's lair. Their expertise is critical in a clan's growth and ultimate strength. Without good builders, a clan's life will be harder than it needs to be.

There are a number of subtypes to the Builder role available to all Builders:

  • Digger
  • Trap Master
  • Fortifier
  • Prospector
  • Sapper

In addition, each race has a Builder subtype available only to them:

  • Undead Grave Robber
  • Humanoid Spelunker
  • Chimera Vein Sniffer
