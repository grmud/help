Summary: General Mechanics: Client


Grendel's Revenge Client

The Grendel's Revenge client (also known as Alice) an ActiveX program that allows you to play the game.

Beneath the links, you will see a tombstone shaped box labeled Stats.

  • Health -- The red bar on the left side of this box displays your current health. When this bar is full, you are at full health. Displayed underneath this bar is your current hit points, then your maximum hit points.
  • Favor Points -- The middle bar, which fills up with a blue color, displays your current favor points. When this bar fills, you will earn a favor.
  • Favors -- When you earn a favor, it will be displayed as an eyeball next to the favor point (blue) bar.
  • Timer -- The rightmost bar is your time bar. When this bar is full, you can perfomr an action (or command). After you have taken an action, this bar will be empty. The bar refills until you are no longer busy, indicating you can perform another.

Beneath the stats box is another tombstone shaped box labeled Resources. All resources that you are carrying will be displayed here.

  • Gold -- The number under the yellow ore icon shows you how much Gold you are carrying.
  • Iron -- The number under the red metal icon shows you how much Iron you are carrying.
  • Shadow -- The number under the black hand icon shows you how much Shadow you are carrying.
  • Mana -- The number under the pink and purple stars icon shows you how much Mana you have.
  • Stone -- The number under the pile of rocks icon shows you how much Stone you have.

The uppermost section of the right side of the client displays your special abilities. Each ability that you know will be displayed here as an icon. You may click on any of these abilities to see more detailed information on them. Beneath the ability icons is your map. Your current position is directly in the center of the map. The map displays the current terrain or lair room along with adjacent rooms. Green arrows on the map show rooms that you can enter. Red arrows show areas that you cannot enter. You may click on a green arrow to travel in that direction.

You may also colorize the text output in your window by using the preferences command.
