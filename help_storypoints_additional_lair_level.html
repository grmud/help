Summary: StoryPoints: Premium Subscribers: Additional Clan Lair Level


Additional Lair level for your Clan

  • Available to: Premium Subscribers
  • Command Usage: lairdepth
  • Cost: 500 Story Points
You can increase the lair depth of any clan you join up to a maximum of twelve levels. The default number of levels to a lair is ten. Therefore, if you decided to spend Story Points on this benefit, you could increase your clan's lair to a maximum of eleven.

If you increase the depth of your clan's lair, you can transfer that benefit to other clans' lairs by just changing clans. The maximum level a clan can increase the depth of its lair is to twelve. However, if you delete this character, you do not receive a refund on the Story Points spent for this benefit, and your clan loses the benefit as well.
