Room/Lair Makeover

  • Available to: Premium Subscribers
  • Requires GM Support
  • Cost: 400 SPs
This includes altering a specific room's appearance and name. This benefit cannot be used to change a room's statistics or functionality in anyway. The benefits are purely cosmetic in description only. Note that this will only cover one room, not an entire lair. Room customizations are dependent on GM availability and approval.
