Summary: Abilities: Leader Special Abilities: Spirituality


 Spirituality

Leaders with spirituality have spent many long hours in meditation, exploring the nature of the universe. The knowledge that they have brought back allows them to direct and influence their clan's spiritual side. Spirituality will cause the monster with it to heal at a greater rate. Any shamen under the monster with Spirituality will see their energy and authority increased, as well as their to-hit chances and defenses.
