Empath

The Empath is in touch with their environment, using their understanding of their place in it to increase the effectiveness of all they do. Their unique understanding of the energy in all of us allow them and their minions to heal at a faster rate. This faster healing, however, taxes the body. Because of this, empaths and their minions will see a decrease in their Energy. Knowing its place in the grand scheme of the universe, the empath and its minions walk with improved Authority.

  • Empaths and their minions have an increased regeneration rate.
  • Empaths and their minions receive a bonus to their authority.
  • Empaths and their minions suffer a penalty to their energy.
