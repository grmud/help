Torture Chamber

A torture chamber is the most feared room in the lair. Devices for causing pain and suffering fill its walls. The fear caused by the torture chamber causes all single role leaders in the clan to receive an increase to their strength and authority. Any grunt inside of the torture chamber will have their morale negatively affected. Torture chambers also increase the amount of passive favor that leaders gain.

Effects that can be gained from a Torture Chamber:

  • The strength and authority of all single role leaders in the clan is increased.
  • The morale of all grunts inside a torture chamber is decreased.
  • The passive favor point gain of all clan leaders is increased.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)
