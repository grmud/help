Hobgoblin

Hobgoblins are humanoids of around five feet in height, with ugly faces and large canines with poke out from their mouth. They are bluff and bravado, their thin skin prone to easy injury, but their intimidating attitude making up for any physical weakness. They are fond of bullying their smaller goblin cousins, and can easily gain control over the weak-minded creatures.

  • Hobgoblins are of medium size.
  • Hobgoblins are resistant to slashing, crushing, and piercing damage.
  • Hobgoblins are vulnerable to poison, acid, and disease.
  • Hobgoblins have a general penalty applied to their offense and defense.
  • Hobgoblins have a penalty applied to their authority and agility.

Note: The offensive, defensive, and attribute penalties are smaller than those for kobolds.
