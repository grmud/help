Crypt

A crypt is a structure only able to be built by the undead. It is the primary defense point of an undead lair. Undead clan members who are inside of a crypt will heal at an even greater rate than usual, and will have their defense increased. Clan grunts on the same level as a crypt will have their defenses increased.

Effects that can be gained from a Crypt:

  • The healing rate of undead clan members who are inside is increased.
  • The defense of all undead clan members who are inside is increased.
  • The defense of all clan grunts on the same level as a crypt is increased.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)
