Shaman Special Abilities

Shaman act as the healers, defenders and support members of a clan. Many of their abilities contribute to this role within a clan. While they may not be the ones heading up battles or rallying troops, their presence is definitely felt by their supportive actions.

Most of the Shamans' actions center around healing, protection, and harnessing the divine energy of Uthgol.
