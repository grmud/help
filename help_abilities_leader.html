Summary: Abilities: Leader Special Abilities


Leader Special Abilities

While a leader's ability in combat is limited and their benefits are not immediately apparent, leaders are still invaluable to a clan. Leaders are the only role that can form or lead a clan.

Leaders special abilities often give benefits to their minions, clan members, and/or grunts. While leaders may not be best combatant in battle, their abilities should make a group of social monsters much more effective in their daily activities.
