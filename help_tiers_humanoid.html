Humanoid Tier

Wandering humanoid monsters begin their lives as kobolds. As they acquire karma and reincarnate, they travel down a long path, becoming larger and stronger with each step. They will soon outstrip their social brethren in power and skill. Each new monster type down the tier will have special features not available to other monsters.

  1. Kobold
  2. Imp or Hobgoblin
  3. Bugbear
  4. Hag or Dwerger
  5. Troglodyte
  6. Ettin or Titan
  7. Cyclops or Giant*

*Note: At tier level seven, a titan may only reincarnate as a giant, and a ettin may only reincarnate as a cyclops. Such is the power of these monsters that only one player may be a giant, and only one player may be a cyclops.
