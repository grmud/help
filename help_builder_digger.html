Digger

Diggers are the quintessential architects, able to plan their buildings more effectively than most. Their grunts work faster than normal, and they can fit more rooms into each floor of their lairs.

  • The grunts of diggers work faster than others.
  • The maximum size of a lair is increased by a digger.
