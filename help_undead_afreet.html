Summary: General Mechanics: Gameplay Mechanics: Castes and Races: Undead: Afreet


The afreet is a burning demon of fire. They are surrounded by flames and burn all the world around them with infernal heat. The afreet has a skeletal body and bright, ruby eyes. The afreet burns the world, making all into ash, converting all objects into fiery remains.
