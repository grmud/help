Imp

Imps are small, demonic creatures with bats wings and sharp teeth. They have long noses out of which hair usually sprouts. They fly about the surface lands as darting horrors, causing panic and pandemonium among their targets. Imps are not very strong, but are quite quick and dextrous.

  • Imps are of small size.
  • Imps receive a bonus to their energy.
  • Imps are resistant to electric, cold, and fire damage.
  • Imps are vulnerable to slashing, crushing, and piercing damage.
  • Imps have a general penalty applied to their offense and defense.
  • Imps have a penalty applied to their authority and agility.

Note: The offensive, defensive, and attribute penalties are smaller than those for kobolds.
