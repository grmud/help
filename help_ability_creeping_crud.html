Summary: Abilities: General Special Abilities: Creeping Crud


 Creeping Crud

The bodies of some monsters swim with infectious disease. Parasites and germs surround them in a cloud of pestilence. Monsters with this ability can enhance their natural attacks by inflicting disease on other creaturesan infection that can be quite deadly to monsters and humans alike.

Command: N/A

This ability is based on:

  • Energy
  • Agility
    • Zombies may purchase the creeping crud ability for half of the normal karma cost.

    Effects of the creeping crud ability:

    • Attacks with the monster's tooth and nail and tail attack abilities will infect the target with disease. The amount of disease caused increases with ranks in creeping crud.
