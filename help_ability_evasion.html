Summary: Abilities: Humanoid Special Abilities: Evasion


 Evasion

Quick and nimble humanoids can dodge the attacks of their enemies. The evasion ability allows monsters to avoid being hit, the first step in staying alive. It also allows humanoids to more easily escape from battle.

Command: N/A

This ability is based on:

  • Agility

    • Evasion may only be purchased by members of the humanoid caste.

    Effects of the evasion ability:

    • Evasion is always in effect, and protects the monster with it from being hit by all types of attacks.
    • Evasion will make it easier to retreat from battle.
    • Investing favors in evasion will increase both of these effects.
