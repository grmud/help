Summary: Abilities: Leader Special Abilities: Warmonger


 Warmonger

The warmonger ability allows a leader to assist its warrior minions in battle. Leaders with this ability enhance the strengths of its warrior minions and increases their combat abilities while the warmonger itself becomes infused with more health. This ability is not particularly useful to chimera, since chimera cannot be warriors and generally do not have warriors as minions.

Command: N/A

This ability is based on:

  • Strength
  • Authority
    • Effects of the warmonger ability:

    • Warmonger increases the maximum hit points of all warriors who are direct minions of the leader.
    • Warmonger will increase the agility and strength of all warriors who are direct minions of the leader.
    • Warmonger will increase the defenses of all warriors who are direct minions of the leader.
    • Warmonger will increase the to-hit chances of all warriors who are direct minions of the leader.
