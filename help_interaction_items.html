Summary: General Mechanics: World Interaction: Item Manipulation


An 'Item' is a general term used to describe any 'thing' (as opposed to a creature, character, or non-player character) with which your character can interact. From containers to clothing, to weapons and food, to rocks and trees, nearly every physical thing in the world can be manipulated in a variety of ways--some obvious, some less so.
