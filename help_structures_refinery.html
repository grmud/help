Refinery

The refinery is the workshop of the clan builders. Here they reinforce building materials, design more efficient building techniques, and practice their craft. Refineries increase the energy and agility of all single role builders in the clan. They also decrease the maintenance cost of all strucutres on the same level. The construction cost of all structures built by the clan is also reduced. The maximum integrity of all structures on the same level as the refinery is increased.

Effects that can be gained from a Refinery:

  • The strength and agility of all single role builders in the clan is increased.
  • The maintenance cost of all structures on the same level as the refinery is decreased.
  • The construction cost of all structures built by clan members is decreased.
  • The maximum integrity of all structures on the same level is increased.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)
