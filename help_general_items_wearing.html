Summary: General Mechanics: World Interaction: Item Manipulation: Wearing


Clothing and armor may be worn by all monsters except those of the Chimera caste. Armor may be worn in the following places:

  • Head
  • Torso
  • Arms
  • Legs
  • Hands
  • Feet
  • Neck
  • Two Rings

Armor will protect you from attack. Generally, the more defense a piece of armor provides, the heavier it is. Heavy armor will slow you down, and make your attacks and use of other abilities less frequent. Armor can take damage in battles. Fire-breathing, cold-breathing, and tail attack are especially good at damaging armor.

Some common commands that pertain to wearing clothing and armor:

  • wear <clothing>
  • remove <clothing>
