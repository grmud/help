Grendel's Revenge Macros

To make this type of macro: type @MACRO and select ADD. You will be first asked to create a shorthand string, this is the shorcut name of whatever action or emote you want to create. Next you will be asked to enter what the macro shorthand should expand to, this is the action or emote that you want to do.

Example 1:

  • Shorthand string: af
  • Expanded macro: approach figher

Example 2:

  • Shorthand string: haunch
  • Expanded macro: emote sits back on his haunches and glares at the sky.

You can ADD, DELETE and LIST any macros you have from the @MACRO command.
