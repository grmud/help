Zombie

Zombies are walking corpses, strong beyond their physical bodies, which are desiccated and bloated. They seek to destroy the living world, to rip apart all life, to spread destruction. Physical damage means little to them as they search out new victims, relentless in their assault. Their bodies swim with the disease of decay, and they can easily infect their opponents with it.

  • Zombies are of medium size.
  • Zombies may purchase the creeping crud ability at half of the normal karma cost.
  • Zombies are resistant to poison, disease, and acid.
  • Zombies are vulnerable to electric, cold, and fire damage.
  • Zombies have a general penalty applied to their offense and defense.
  • Zombies have a penalty applied to their strength and agility.

Note: The offensive, defensive, and attribute penalties are smaller than those for spirits.

Zombies may reincarnate to the following monster types:
