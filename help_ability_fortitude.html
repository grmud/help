Summary: Abilities: General Special Abilities: Fortitude


 Fortitude

With long hours of athletic exertion and training, a monster can greatly increase the strength and durability of its physical body. A monster with this ability will be able to sustain much more damage before succumbing to the throes of death.

Command: N/A

This ability is based on:

  • Strength

    • Effects of the fortitude ability:

    • Each rank in fortitude will add maximum hit points.
