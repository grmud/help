Summary: Abilities: General Special Abilities: Hitcher


 Hitcher

Many creatures can be rode by a monster who knows how to handle the saddle. This ability allows to ride a creature or a fellow monster without danger of falling off. If mounting another monster, you must have their permission for this skill to work. For this ability to work, its target must also be bigger than yourself.

Command: N/A

This ability is based on:

  • Agility
  • Strength
