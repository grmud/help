Summary: General Mechanics: Gameplay Mechanics: Social Monsters: Roles: Warriors


Warriors are the rank and file of the monster world. All clans rely upon them for their freedom and their expansion. They range from the tactician to the killing machine, but all are focused on one thing - littering the ground with the fallen bodies of their foes.

There are a number of subtypes to the warrior role, available to all Warriors regardless of race:

  • Strategist
  • Savage
  • Hunter
  • Martial Artist
  • Champion

In addition, each race has a warrior subtype available only to them (excluding chimera):

  • Undead Plague Terror
  • Humanoid Soldier
