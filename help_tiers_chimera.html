Chimera Tier

Wandering chimera monsters begin their lives as scarabs. As they acquire karma and reincarnate, they travel down a long path, becoming larger and stronger with each step. They will soon outstrip their social brethren in power and skill. Each new monster type down the tier will have special features not available to other monsters.

  1. Scarab
  2. Salamander or Barghest
  3. Grimalkin
  4. Hippogryph or Roc
  5. Cerebus
  6. Basilisk or Hydra
  7. Rakshasa or Dragon*

*Note: At tier level seven, a basilisk may only reincarnate as a rakshasa, and a hyrdra may only reincarnate as a dragon. Such is the power of these monsters that only one player may be a rakshasa, and only one player may be a dragon.
