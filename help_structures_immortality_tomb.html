Immortality Tomb

An immortality tomb may only be built by an undead grave robber. Immortality Tombs are places steeped in the nature of undeath, where undead may have their essence reinforced. Undead may sleep here for a time, granting them some protection against the ravages of the sun. They may also defile the corpses of their enemies, increasing their connection to the cycle of life and death. Finally, they may also summon wards against enemies who might disturb their rest or work.

Effects that can be gained from an Immortality Tomb:

  • When an undead sleeps at its clan's immortality tomb, it gains a resistance to the effects of sunlight for a short period of time.
  • When an undead desecrates corpses at its clan's immortality tomb, it may gains a small amount of karma.
  • When an undead activates its clan's immortality tomb defenses, enemies of the clan may not easily enter the tomb.
(Note: The effects of these abilities increase as monsters put favor into the structure.)

Commands:

  • sleep -- instill a temporary resistance to the sun.
  • desecrate -- destroy a corpse, in an effort to gain karma.
  • defend -- block enemies of the clan who attempt to enter.
