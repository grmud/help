Ghost

Ghosts are spirits who seek escape from the physical world, but are never granted exit. They are frozen images, smoky wisps of terror. In constant pain caused by their inability to escape, they seek to torment any living being they can find.

  • Ghosts are of medium size.
  • Ghosts may purchase the noncorporeal ability at half of the normal karma cost.
  • Ghosts are extremely resistant to cold damage.
  • Ghosts are vulnerable to electric and fire damage.
  • Ghosts have a general penalty applied to their offense and defense.
  • Ghosts have a penalty applied to their strength and energy.

Note: The offensive, defensive, and attribute penalties are smaller than those for spirits.

Ghosts may reincarnate to the following monster types:
