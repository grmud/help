Summary: Abilities: Magical Special Abilities: Poison Breath


 Poison Breath

Using this ability, the monster exhales a large cloud of poison gas, which can affect multiple targets. Poisoned monsters will be damaged physically and the poison will remain in their systems, further hurting them over time.

Command: pois?onbreath <target>

This ability is based on:

  • Energy
    • Poison breathing may only be purchased by monsters that have magical as their only role.

    Effects of the poison breathing ability:

    • Poison breathing is a long ranged attack that poisons its targets. At high levels, it can damage more than one target.
    • Investing favors in poison breathing will increase the level of poison it causes, and add the ability to splash to additional targets.
