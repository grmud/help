Alpha

The alpha leader is not only driven to be the best in their clan, but requires that their clan to be the best amongst all the clans. Their arrogance (some say insecurity) gives them defense bonuses for each Minion they lead, and in turn their Minions combat bonuses for being led by them. However, they do not tend to make good magic-users, and have trouble recovering from battle.

  • Alphas gain a percentage bonus to their fighting capabilities, affected by their grumble size.
  • Alphas' minions gain a bonus to their defense.
  • Alphas acquire a penalty to their energy.
