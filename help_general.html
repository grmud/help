Summary: General Mechanics


The Isle of Uthgol, where Grendel's Revenge takes place, is rich and complex, but we have tried to make the interface between it and you, as a player, easy to use and as intuitive as possible. For the most part, interaction with the world is command-driven: you supply a verb and a target (or targets) and the system does the rest. What follows is a list of help topics that cover the mechanical aspects of the game--how things work in the game, and how you can get them to do what you want.
