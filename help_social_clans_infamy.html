Infamy

Infamy represents a monster's reputation - How well known the monster is to other monsters and to uglies alike.

Infamy increases by a number of methods. Infamy is initially based off of a monster's influence. Many actions will increase the infamy of a monster. Killing humans and other monsters increases your infamy, as does destroying structures.

Clans also have an infamy. As the members of a clan increase in infamy, the clan's total infamy also increases. Clan infamy determines how many adventurers will raid your lair, and how strong they will be.

Individual monster infamy determines which adventurers will attack which monsters. Monsters with a higher infamy will find themselves to be the target of adventurers, as adventurers seek to make a name for themselves by killing well known monsters.
