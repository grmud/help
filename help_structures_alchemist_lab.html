Alchemist Lab

The alchemist lab is the home of the clan's magical monsters. Here they can research new spells, work on potion recipies, and train their skills. Alchemist labs provide a bonus to energy for all clan members who have magical as their first role. They also provide bonuses to the to-hit chance and damage of all clan member magical attacks.

Effects that can be gained from an Alchemist Lab:

  • The energy of all clan members with magical as their first role is increased.
  • The damage of all clan members' magical attacks is increased.
  • The to-hit chance of all clan members' magical attacks is increased.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)
