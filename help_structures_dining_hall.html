Dining Hall

The Dining Hall provides nourishment for all clan members and grunts. The cost to summon grunts from breeding pits built near a dining hall will be decreased. Any clan members who are on the same level as a dining hall will have their maximum hit points increased due to the nourishing effects of nearby food. As favors are invested in this structure, the maximum amount of grunts each clan member can control will be increased, and the statistics of all grunts on the same level as the dining hall will increase.

Effects that can be gained from a Dining Hall:

  • The cost of grunts summoned in Breeding Pits near the Dining Hall is decreased.
  • Clan members on the same level as the Dining Hall will have their maximum hit points increased.
  • The maximum number of grunts that each clan member can control will be increased.
  • The statistics of all grunts on the same level as the Dining Hall will be increased.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)
