Summary: General Mechanics: Gameplay Mechanics: Social Monsters


Social Monsters are the communal monsters are weak by themselves but pose a great threat when found in large numbers. The benefit of playing a social monster is that they bonuses for banding together (by forming clans), they can specialize in certain aspects of the game by taking on certain roles, they can build lairs underground to support their social bonds, and order smaller, stupider cousins, commonly referred to as "grunts." However, they are limited by their race, and individually they can not become very powerful.
