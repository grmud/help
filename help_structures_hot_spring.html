Hot Springs

Hot Springs, Quagmires, and Grottos are relaxing structures which provide clan members with sanctuaries against the outside world. Clan members who are inside the hot springs will heal faster. Monsters not in the clan will be burned and take damage while inside the hot springs. Clan members may also slurp from the hot springs to gain health.

Effects that can be gained from a Hot Springs:

  • While clan members are inside the hot springs, they heal at an increased rate.
  • While non clan members are inside the hot springs, they take continual damage.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)

Commands usable at a Hot Springs:

  • slurp -- Slurping in a hot springs will heal any clan member who does it.

Caste-specific Names:
Undead: Quagmire
Humanoid: Hot Spring
Chimera: Grotto
