Stairs

Building staircases allow a clan to build their lair deeper into the earth. In order to build down to a lower level using a staircase, first build a staircase on the level you wish to build down from. You will then be able to build down from that staircase.

Caste-specific Names:
Undead: creepy staircase
Humanoid: flight of steps
Chimera: dug-out ramp

Building Costs:
Gold: 25
Stone: 250
Caste: 25

Maintenance Costs:
Gold: 2
Stone: 5
Caste: 1
