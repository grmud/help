Playing an NPC

  • Available to: Premium Subscribers
  • Cost: See Below
Premium Subscribers can use Story Points to assume the roles of NPCs and creatures in the gameworld. While the NPCs will be persistent and occupy a character slot, they can die only once, and can't be brought back to life.
The list of available NPCs:
  • Druid - 50 SPs
  • Bandit - 80 SPs
  • Mystic - 110 SPs
  • Knight - 140 SPs
  • Monk - 170 SPs
  • Bard - 200 SPs
  • Elementalist - 230 SPs
  • Ranger - 260 SPs
  • Demon Hunter - 290 SPs
  • Corsair - 320 SPs
  • Warlock - 350 SPs
  • *Slayer - 380 SPs
  • *Hierophant - 410 SPs
  • *Assassin - 440 SPs
  • *Sorcerer - 470 SPs
  • *Paladin - 500 SPs
* not yet available
