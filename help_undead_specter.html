Specter

The specter is the image behind the mirror, the unreal being who stalks just outside of its target's vision. Specters are embodied in a field of cold energy. Their screams have the ability to paralyze their victims, stunning them with fear. Specters hate fire and the warm, daytime world.

  • Specters are of medium size.
  • Specters may purchase the stun ability at half of the normal karma cost.
  • Specters are nearly immune to cold damage.
  • Specters are very vulnerable to electric and fire damage.
  • Specters have a general penalty applied to their offense and defense.
  • Specters have a penalty applied to their strength and energy.

Note: The offensive, defensive, and attribute penalties are smaller than those for zombies and ghosts.

Specters may reincarnate to the following monster types:

Ghost Zombie
