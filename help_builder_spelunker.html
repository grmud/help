Spelunker

Only humanoid builders can be spelunkers. Spelunkers are at home in the darkness of the caverns, and are unusually good at tracking down sources of iron ore. They are uniquely able to build Slime Pits, which produce a substance that gives those who bathe in the pit a large bonus to their natural armor and strength. Spelunkers are notorious for their lackadaisical attitude towards healing and assembly structures however, leading to extra expense in building them.

  • Spelunkers with the basic structures ability may build Slime Pits.
  • The grunts of Spelunkers mine iron at an enhanced rate.
  • Spelunkers pay an increased cost to build certain structures.
