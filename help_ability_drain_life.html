Summary: Abilities: Warrior Special Abilities: Drain Life


 Drain Life

Monsters with the drain life ability can suck the life essence from their victim's bodies. The target of drain life will see its abilities and statistics reduced in power. Drain life can also remove hit points, adding the drained hit points to the monster using the ability.

Command: drain <character>
