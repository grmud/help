Summary: Abilities: Shaman Special Abilities: Divination


 Divination

By studying the art of divination, a shamen can see far across space and time. Far away places may be spied on, detailed information may be gained about enemy structures and movements. Entire enemy lairs may be detected, as well as human towns and structures.

Command: spy <directions>

This ability is based on:

  • Energy
