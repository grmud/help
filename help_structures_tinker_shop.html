Tinker Shop

Tinker shops are the home and workshop of the clan trappers. Here they can practice their craft, honing their trapping skills. The presence of a tinker shop in a lair will cause clan traps to do more damage and cost less. Clan members with the ability to detect/disarm traps will have their detecting and disarming skills increased.

Effects that can be gained from a tinker shop.

  • All clan traps will do more damage.
  • All clan trappers will have their ability to detect and disarm traps increased.
  • All clan traps will cost less to construct.
(Note: Not all of these effects will be available when the structure is first built. As favors are invested in the structure, more effects will become available. For each favor invested in the structure, the strength of each of these effects will also be increased.)
