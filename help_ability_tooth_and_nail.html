Summary: Abilities: General Special Abilities: Tooth and Nail


 Tooth and Nail

All monsters are granted this ability on creation. This ability represents a monster's ability to attack with its natural weapons. Whether the monster uses claws, fangs, or simply clenched fists, all monsters can attack their opponents in melee combat with this ability.

Command: claw <character>

This ability is based on:

  • Strength
  • Agility

    • Effects of the tooth and nail ability:

    • Tooth and nail is given to all monsters for free upon creation. Tooth and nail is a melee attack that does claw damage.
    • Tooth and nail may be enhanced with the iron claw, venom, and creeping crud abilities.

    Blood Oath | Creeping Crud | Fortitude | Venom
