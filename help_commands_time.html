Summary: Commands: Time


Usage: time

Time passes in the world of Uthgol. It will always either be night or day. You can use the time command to estimate the current time of day.You cannot see what time of day it is while you are inside or underground.

Undead are greatly affected by the presence of the sun in the sky. During the day, all undead characters will see their abilities decline.
