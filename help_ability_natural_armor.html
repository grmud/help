Summary: Abilities: Chimera Special Abilities: Natural Armor


 Natural Armor

Whether thick hide, scales, or feathers, many monsters have a natural protection against the ravages of the outside world. This ability gives a monster an built-in suit of armor, which will help prevent it from taking damage.

Command: N/A

This ability is based on:

  • Strength
  • Energy

    • Natural Armor may only be purchased by members of the chimera caste.

    Effects of the natural armor ability:

    • Natural Armor is always in effect, and protects the monster with it from being hit by all types of attacks.
    • Investing favors in natural armor will increase the protection.
