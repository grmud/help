Burrow

A burrow is the Chimera's place to hunker down, rest and relax within the lair. The burrow entices harpy grunts from their Hatcheries and instills a protective aura of magic around the Chimera clan. Many expert monster builders believe that the unique formation of the structure and the placement of its exits in the right direction give this structure its unique abilities, but no one knows for sure.

Effects that can be gained from a burrow:

  • Burrows reduce the cost of summoning grunts while the summoning monster is within the clan lair.
  • Burrows increase the storage capacity of mana that a clan can hold in its storage chambers.
  • Burrows also give Chimera members resistance to magical attacks.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)
