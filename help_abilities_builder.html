Builder Special Abilities

Builders are responsible for the architecture and construction of a clan's lair. Their expertise is critical in a clan's growth and ultimate strength. Builders give a clan the ability and functionality with which to create a lair, maintain it, repair it, and protect it. That is why a clan is required to have builders to even construct the lair.

A builder's special abilities focus on trap placement, disarmament and detection, building various structures, mining, and general lair maintenance.
