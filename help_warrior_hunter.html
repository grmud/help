Hunter

Hunters are excellent trackers, and can teach their grunts how to do so as well, though this focus on extra skills reduces the number of grunts they can effectively oversee. Their endurance, derived from hours spent on the trail of prey, also leads to an increased resistance to degenerative damage.

  • Hunters are resistant to poison, acid, and disease.
  • Hunters and their minions receive a bonus to agility.
  • Hunters may command less grunts than other monsters.
