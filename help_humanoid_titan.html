Summary: General Mechanics: Gameplay Mechanics: Castes and Races: Humanoid: Titan


The titan is a huge humanoid which bears little resemblence to any of its kin. Titans are generally regarded as very ugly by most other monsters. They bear a distinct resemblence to pink-skins, but with hair and eyes of blue, green, purple, or any other color. Titans are very strong and natural boulder throwers.
