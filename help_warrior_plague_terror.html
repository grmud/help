Plague Terror

Only undead warriors can be plague terrors. The plague terror is feared by friend and foe alike. The Terror's Minions are able to better resist degenerative damage through their familiarity with its constantly sapping effects, though their Energy and Authority are lowered by their association.

  • Plague Terrors and their minions have a resistance to disease, poison, and acid.
  • Plague Terrors have penalties applied to their energy and agility.
