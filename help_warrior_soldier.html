Soldier

Only humanoid warriors can be soldiers. Soldiers are professional about their combat, obsessing over their equipment, and wanting to be sure everything is done correctly. This care allows their equipment can receive double the normal amount of enchantments, and ensures that it is in top shape, doing extra damage. They are slower in their attack rates though, wanting to be just that little be more careful of their equipment, in case there is a surprise inspection after the fight.

  • Soldiers receive a bonus to the damage they cause.
  • Soldiers suffer an increase to their attack roundtimes.
  • Soldiers have a bonus to hit with missile weapons.
