Summary: General Mechanics: Gameplay Mechanics: Castes and Races: Humanoid: Cyclops


The Cyclops is a gigantic one-eyed humanoid. Cyclops have a natural connection to the forces of electricity. They can harness this sky-power to pull lightning bolts from the sky and hurl them at enemies. The cyclop's vulnerability is its eye, which is very prone to being pierced. There is only one cyclops, one smith of the storm.
