Emotes

For all you do-it-yourself types out there and for all those times when a simple "smile" isn't enough, the "act" command (":", for short) will let you create your own pose or dramatization. This command basically takes whatever you type after it, sticks a space in it and displays it for all to see with your name in front of it. Use it carefully.

What comes out is expressed in the third person, so it is helpful to make sure that what you type is also expressed in that tense.

Usage: act <text> or :<text> or emote <text>

:growls at Mangefur angrily.
Everyone sees: "Neckgar growls at Mangefur angrily."
act flaps her wings and soars into the air.
Everyone sees: "Neckgar flaps her wings and soars into the air."
