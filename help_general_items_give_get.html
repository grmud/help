Giving/Getting

Monsters love to carry hoard. It's an undeniable fact that the more crowns, swords, gold and mana hanging out of your pockets and adorning your body, the more powerful of a monster you are. Here's a little advice on how to collect, stow and trade (if your master forces you to) your loot.

Some common commands that pertain to giving/getting:

  • get/take <what> -- If you want to get an item off the ground or nearby
  • get/take from <what> -- You can acquire an item from another object (like a corpse)
  • loot <storage> or <corpse> -- Allows you to loot resources (gold, stone, mana, shadow or iron) off a corpse of a dead foe or from a storage chamber in a lair.
  • -- Allows you to offer an item to a specific monster
  • accept <who> -- If another creature offers you an item, you can refuse it or accept it
  • get my <what> -- Specifically will check only your inventory for the specified item.
  • stop -- Stops your current activity (such as offering)
