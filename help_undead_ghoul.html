Ghoul

Ghouls are, first and foremost, scavengers. They consume the rotting flesh of corpses, and this keeps their own rotting bodies unnaturally strong, quick and agile. The concept of the Pack is very important to ghouls; they are excellent trackers and prefer to overwhelm their victims with sheer numbers. Each member of the pack knows its job well, and together they strike swiftly from the shadows, stalking their prey and driving it into panicked, futile flight before bringing it down in a lethal hail of claws and teeth.

With their strong claws they can swiftly dig deep burrows in the earth to hide from the hated sun, dragging the rotted bodies of their night's hunting in behind them. Ghouls can understand simple concepts related to hunting, killing, and digging, but they are not inclined to much or deep thought. The joys of their undead lives are simple ones; stalking, chasing, eating.

In appearance, ghouls are humanoid in form, though their bodies are perpetually rotting. They are bestial in movement, quick and furtive, sliding from shadow to shadow. They have a mouth full of pointed yellow or black teeth, long thick claws, and tough, scabbed skin. They care little for their appearance and are inclined to smell like grave rot and old meat.

  • Ghouls are of medium size.
  • Ghouls may purchase the corpse feast ability at half of the normal karma cost.
  • Ghouls are vulnerable to cold damage.
  • Ghouls are resistant to poison.
  • Ghouls have a penalty applied to their authority.
