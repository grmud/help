Upgrading Structures

Social Monsters may upgrade and add favor to structures within their lair. Upgrades benefit the structure by increasing the effects the structure grants to the clan and its members, and by increasing the integrity of a structure. For the possible effects of each structure, see the help node on that particular structure:
  • Basic Lair Structures
  • Advanced Lair Structures
  • Caste Specific Structures
Upgrading a structure is a two part process:
  1. First a monster needs to add favor to the structure, which any clan member that has reached level five or higher, regardless of role, can do. To spend a favor on a structure, you must stand in the structure you wish to upgrade, and type the command: favor. A window will open, giving you the option to spend favor on the structure.
  2. Next, to receive the benefits of the favor, a builder within the clan, who has the ability to build structures, must go and upgrade the structure. To upgrade the structure, the builder commands the grunt to 'upgrade', and the grunt will use the pending favors to do the structure upgrades.
To view the power or status of a structure, monsters can use the upgrades command. This command will give information on the current structure's integrity, it maximum possible integrity, the number of favors that have been converted on a structure, and the number of favors available for conversion. And if a structure is in the process of being upgraded, this command will also indicate how much of the upgrade has been completed.
