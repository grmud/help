Summary: Abilities: Builder Special Abilities: Mining


 Mining

The mining skill is required to build a mine. Mines are the main source of wealth for clans. Monsters with the mining ability can also learn to build a number of basic and advanced structures as they invest favor in it. The mining ability will cause grunts to mine faster, mine more resources, and will increase the storage capacity of a clan's storage chambers. Miners also have access to the 'mine' command, which allow them to directly dig out resources from the clan's mines.

Command: mine

This ability is based on:

  • Agility
  • Energy
