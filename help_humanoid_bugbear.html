Bugbear

Bugbears are the nightmare creatures used by mothers to frighten their children. They are bearlike creatures covered in thick lairs of matted hair. They have bright eyes, and long, viscious beaks. They are fond of stabbing weapons, delighting in impaling their victims.

  • Bugbears are of medium size.
  • Bugbears may purchase the stab ability at half of the normal karma cost.
  • Bugbears are resistant to slashing, crushing, and piercing damage.
  • Bugbears are vulnerable to poison, acid, and disease.
  • Bugbears have a general penalty applied to their offense and defense.
  • Bugbears have a penalty applied to their authority and agility.

Note: The offensive, defensive, and attribute penalties are smaller than those for imps and hobgoblins.

Bugbears may reincarnate to the following monster types:
