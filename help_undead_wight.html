Summary: General Mechanics: Gameplay Mechanics: Castes and Races: Undead: Wight


The wight is the spirit of a warrior or priest preserved for eternity in a dead body. Wights are driven by purpose, remaining in this world to accomplish deeds they were unable to complete in life. The dead body of the wight can easily resist physical damage. Wights have little connection to the magical plane, and dislike noise, preferring the silence of purpose.
