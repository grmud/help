Summary: StoryPoints: Premium Subscribers: Starting a Wandering Monster at Tier


Starting a Wandering Monster at a higher tier

  • Available to: Premium Subscribers
  • Command Usage: changeform <tier number>
  • Cost: 400 for Tier 2, 800 for Tier 3
You have the option of spending Story Points to skip to either tier two or three for as a Wandering Monster, by using the same command, changeform (see above), that you would normally use to reincarnate into a new monster.

There are some limitations to this benefit. First, you cannot be higher than level five when you skip to a new Wandering Monster tier and you will lose all the favors you earned before you skipped to that tier.
