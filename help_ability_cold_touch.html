Summary: Abilities: Magical Special Abilities: Cold Touch


 Cold Touch

The freezing grasp of a monster using the cold touch ability will chill any creature to the bone. This ability inflicts cold damage on its target.

Command: coldt?ouch <character>

This ability is based on:

  • Energy

    • Spirits may purchase the cold touch ability for half of the normal karma cost.

    Effects of the cold touch ability:

    • Cold touch is a melee attack that does cold damage.
