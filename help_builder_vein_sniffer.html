Vein Sniffer

Only chimera builders can be vein sniffers. Vein sniffers are attuned to mana flows, are are very good at gathering it. They are not so good at building martial and commander buildings though, tending to be sloppy in their plans, leading to more expense in their construction than strictly necessary. They are able to build burrows, decreasing the cost of producing new grunts as well as increasing the effect of their personal hoards on the clan's overall influence.

Chimeras who have the vein sniffer role are able to detect the presence and direction of a resource deposit. To do this, type veinsniff <direction> However, while in a lair or at the ground level, this ability only gives the vein sniffer the ability to find resource deposits at the current level, not above or below them.

  • Vein Sniffers with the basic structures ability can build Burrows.
  • Vein Sniffers can sniff out resource deposits.
  • The grunts of Vein Sniffers mine mana at an enhanced rate.
  • Vein Sniffers pay an increased cost to build certain structures.
