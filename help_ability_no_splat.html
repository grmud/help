Summary: Abilities: General Special Abilities: No Splat


 No Splat

Falling is dangerous. Falling from a great distance can be very painful. Being dropped from a great distance by a malicious dragon can hurt a lot. This skill allows the monster to reduce the damage taken from a fall, by holding its body in the correct position.

Command: N/A
