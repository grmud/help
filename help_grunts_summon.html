Acquiring and Summoning Grunts

Use the commands outlined below to acquire a grunt. Generally, most monsters are limited to only commanding one grunt, unless they have a special ability or structure that allows them to command more (like Grunt Mastery).
  • summon grunt -- You can spend resources while at your clan's breeding pit, and a grunt will be born into your command.
  • summon warrior -- (Warriors & Leaders only) For an increased cost of summoning a normal grunt, you can summon a grunt that is much better at fighting and has battle capabilities.
  • donate -- If you are at Grendel's Shrine, you can donate gold and your caste resource to Grendel and receive a grunt.
  • animate <corpse> -- If you are undead and have the 'Raise Dead' special ability, you can animate a corpse to become a skeleton grunt.
