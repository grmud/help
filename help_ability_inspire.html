Summary: Abilities: Leader Special Abilities: Inspire


 Inspire

A leader with this ability is able to greatly inspire its subordinates when it is engaged in combat. Its brave followers will be much more effective when following their leader into battle, a song on their lips and pride in their hearts.

Command: N/A

This ability is based on:

  • Strength
  • Authority
    • Effects of the inspire ability:

    • While you are engaged in combat, and your minions are engaged in combat, and you are within a certain distance of your minions, they will receive a to-hit bonus in combat.
    • Investing favors in inspire will increase this bonus, and increase the distance you can be from your minions for the bonus to take effect.
