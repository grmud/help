Summary: General Mechanics: World Interaction: Item Manipulation: Parser


The parser will allow your character to select from an unlimited number of items (e.g., get 20 torch (takes the 20th torch in your environment). This works for all commands and verbs. You can 'open 2 door' or 'wink 3 soldier'.

The parser orders your character's environment according to the following priority:

  1. Other Users
  2. Things in Hand
  3. Things Worn
  4. Things in Room

Currently, the parser matches all parts of an object's name, so 'get leath' could find 'a leather sack' or 'a piece of leather' depending on which one was first in your environment.

A useful feature built into this system is the ability to reference something with the word "my." This will override the usual parser and only attempt to match things that are in your inventory.
