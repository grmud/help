Despot

Only undead leaders can be despots. The despot is able to draw upon the power of Shadow to reanimate the fallen. This relationship with shadow is costly to maintain, requiring a greater investment than usual in resources.

  • Despots and their minions receive a large bonus to their maximum hit points when inside an undead lair.
  • Despots may purchase the raise dead ability at half of the normal karma cost.
  • Despots have an increased upkeep cost.
