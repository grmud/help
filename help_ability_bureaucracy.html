Summary: Abilities: Leader Special Abilities: Bureaucracy


 Bureaucracy

Leaders with the bureaucracy ability are master organizers, able to improve the efficiency of the clan and all of the clan's leaders. The strength and authority of all leaders underneath a monster with bureaucracy will be increased. The maximum size of both the clan and the lair will be increased by this ability. The offense and defense of all leaders underneath the monster with this ability will also be improved.

Command: N/A

This ability is based on:

  • Strength
  • Authority
    • Bureaucracy may only be purchased by monsters that have leader as their only role.

    Effects of the bureaucracy ability:

    • Bureaucracy will increase the maximum size of a clan the leader is in.
    • Bureaucracy will increase the maximum size of the lair of a clan the leader is in.
    • Bureaucracy will increase the strength and authority of the leader, and the strength and authority of all leaders directly under the leader.
    • Bureaucracy will increase the defenses of the leader, and the defenses of all leaders directly under it.
    • Bureaucracy will increase the to-hit chances of the leader, and the to-hit chances of all leaders directly under it.
