Clan Hierarchies

All clans are organized into hierarchies, which branch downwards from the leader of the clan. Clan members under the clan leader can have members underneath them, and those members may have members underneath them, and so on down the hierarchy.

Clan Lists

To see the members of your clan who are currently online, you can type 'who clan'. To see your master and your minions, you can type 'minions'. To see the list of all the minions in your clan, you can type 'minions all'.
