Summary: Abilities: Humanoid Special Abilities: Hide


 Hide

Some monsters are perfectly adept at blending in with their surroundings. The hide ability allows a monster to lurk in a room unseen and sneak around without attracting the attention of nearby enemies. A monster must have this ability to use the sneak attack special ability.

Command: hide

This ability is based on:

  • Agility
    • Sneaking

    If you try to move around while hiding, you will be revealed. However, you can use the sneak command to move around instead of automatically revealing yourself to your enemies.

    You may also deliberately reveal yourself by using the unhide command.
