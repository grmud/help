Clans

Command Summary:

  • invite <character> -- Invites a monster to become your minion. You must be within the same location to invite someone to become your minion.
  • accept <character> -- If another monster invites you to become its minion, you can accept its offer.
  • decline <character> -- If another monster invites you to become its minion, you can decline its offer.
  • discharge <master> or <minion> -- Use these commands either to remove one of the minions within your clan, or you can discharge your master, removing yourself from the clan.
  • found clan -- If your clan meets all the requirements outlined below you can form a new clan.
  • name clan <name> -- If you are the leader of your clan, you can use this command to rename it.
  • who clan -- Displays the list of all the members in your clan or grumble who are currently online.
  • minions [all] -- [all] is optional. Displays a list of all your minions and your master. If the [all] is attached to the end of the command, you will see a list of all the members in your clan.
  • grumble <text> You can communicate only with the members of your clan by using this command.

Clans are the backbone of the social structure in Grendel's Revenge. However, only social monsters are allowed to join clans. A clan is a group of social monsters who have sworn allegiance to a master and who work together to form a community, build a lair, and protect each other from the Uglies. Before forming a clan you must first form a grumble (see Grumbles for more information).

A social monster gains many benefits from being a clan member.

  • Clan members gain the benefits of the various structures that are built within their lair.
  • Masters bestow certain abilities on those members within their minion hierarchy.
  • Clan members may control and command grunts to do various tasks for them.

Clan Formation

In order to form a clan:
  1. You must, first have the Leader role.
  2. Then you must have as part of your clan, at least five individual monsters that make up your grumble. Your grumble is all the monsters directly below you in your hierarchy tree: (called your 'minions'), the monsters below them, and on down.
  3. You can finally declare a clan when you have at least five monsters in your entire grumble (see Founding Clans and Lairs for more information).
