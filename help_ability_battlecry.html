Summary: Abilities: Leader Special Abilities: Battlecry


 Battlecry

The battlecry of a monster is enough to make the most downtrodden of allies rally around it. The use of this ability gives courage to all allied monsters who hear it, making them fight with new power and vigor.

Command: bellow

This ability is based on:

  • Strength
  • Authority

    • Effects of the battlecry ability:

    • Battlecry will affect all monsters in your clan or a friendly clan who hear it.
    • Battlecry will increase the hit chances of any monster inspired by it, including the user.
