Wound Licker

Only chimera shaman can be wound lickers. Naturally resistant to magical damage, wound lickers are more effective when healing other Chimera, usually because other races aren't always that keen on getting saliva all over them. The healing properties of their saliva is much less effective upon Undead.

  • Wound Lickers are very good at using the heal ability on chimera.
  • Wound Lickers are not very good at using the heal ability on undead.
