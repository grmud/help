Summary: Abilities: Chimera Special Abilities: Tail Attack


 Tail Attack

Many chimera are blessed with strong, lengthy tails. This ability allows them to use their tails in combat, without needing to approach their enemies.

Command: tail?whip <character>

This ability is based on:

  • Agility
  • Strength

    • Tail Attack may only be purchased by members of the chimera caste.

    Effects of the tail attack ability:

    • Tail attack is a short range attack that does claw damage. You do not need to approach a target to tail attack it.
    • Tail attack may be enhanced with the iron claw, venom, and creeping crud abilities.
