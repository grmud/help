Temple

The temple is the spiritual center of the lair. It provides a place for shamans to perform their rituals and for clan members to worship Grendel. The temple is a sanctuary, and it may be warded against the intrusions of humans. The strength of the temple increases the authority and energy of all single role shaman in the clan. It also increases the effectiveness of all clan shaman's ability to heal.

Effects that can be gained from a Temple:

  • The authority and energy of all single role shaman in the clan is increased.
  • The effects of all clan shaman's healing ability are increased.
(Note: Not all these effects are available right away, but they will be granted as monsters put favor into the structure. The effects of each ability will increase as well.)

Commands useable at a Temple:

  • defend -- This will ward the temple against humans for a period of time. This period of time increases with the amount of favors invested in the structure.
